About Me

Hello! I'm Eduardo (Joey) C. Albano Jr. and this is my webpage. Here you'll find information about me, projects that I'm working on, and ways to contact me.

I graduated from UCSD in 2015 and dropped out of Santa Clara University's School of Law soon after. Since then, I've taken it upon myself to learn programming and computer science to pursue a career in software engineering! I hope to be involved in finance or cryptography -- my dream is to one day design software around cryptocurrencies/blockchain technology.

When I'm not working on things, I love working out (powerlifting), playing games (Melee and DOTA 2 mostly), and eating spicy food. I'm also very interested in Mathematics and languages; right now I'm trying to learn Vietnamese! From time to time I like making music as well, which you can find on my SoundCloud.

As both a part-time gig and hobby, I tutor in Mathematics as well as a variety of standardized tests. You can find more about that here.

My resume can be found here. It's...a work in progress!

Tutoring

I started tutoring in 2014 by helping my college peers with upper division mathematics coursework. I've since branched out to teaching a variety of standardized tests as well. I offer tutoring in the following subjects:

  • Mathematics: up to and including undergraduate level mathematics.
  • Standardized Testing: LSAT, SAT, SAT II Math, APUSH, AP World, AP Calculus.

My usual rate is $35/hour for all subjects. All first-time clients get the first hour free of charge.


Feel free to e-mail me if you have any questions or inquiries about tutoring. References and other credentials are available upon request!
